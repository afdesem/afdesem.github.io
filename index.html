Artificial intelligence has severely impacted in human's daily lives, whether it is for work purposes, studies, researches, or even assistance. First, we ought to know what really is ai from its definition. Although there has been a debate upon the mere definition of AI, The High-Level Expert Group on Artificial intelligence have provided us from a fair starting point.

“Artificial intelligence (AI) refers to systems that display intelligent behaviour by analysing their environment and taking actions – with some degree of autonomy – to achieve specific goals. AI-based systems can be purely software-based, acting in the virtual world (e.g. voice assistants, image analysis software, search engines, speech and face recognition systems) or AI can be embedded in hardware devices (e.g. advanced robots, autonomous cars, drones or Internet of Things applications).”

That said, we could conclude that Artificial Intelligence are made merely to decide and act to achieve upon givem goals.
